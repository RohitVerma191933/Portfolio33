The Central University of Haryana (CUH) was established in the year 2009 and is located in Mahendergarh, Haryana. The university has been recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC. It aims to develop enlightened citizenship of a knowledge society for the prosperity and peace of the globe. The university offers Undergraduate courses in Engineering and Postgraduate courses in various streams like Arts, Science, and Commerce. Admissions at Central University of Haryana courses are done on several entrance exams conducted at the national and state levels.

Central University of Haryana admission 2022 is open for UG and PG courses. Admissions to UG courses will be done through CUCET UG. CUET UG 2022 admit card for phase 2 is out now. Candidates can download the same through the official website - cuet.samarth.ac.in. The exam for Phase 2 is scheduled to be conducted from August 4-6 and August 12-14. Admit Cards for the examinations to be held after August 6, 2022, will be issued later. Candidates seeking admission to PG courses are required to appear for the CUET PG 2022 exam. The exam will be held from September 1-7 and September 9-11.
